About Us

Founded in 1970, Sri Venkateshwara Auto Electrical and Battery has been a trusted name in Chitradurga for over five decades. We are proud to have served generations of customers with integrity, technical excellence, and personalized service.

Our business began as a modest auto electrical shop and has grown into a complete power solution provider. Today, we offer high-quality automotive batteries, UPS systems, inverter batteries, solar panel solutions, and auto electrical services. Our customer relationships are built on transparency, value, and trust.

We are authorized distributors of Microtek and Powerzone, and we are also connected with all other major battery and solar brands to ensure every customer finds exactly what they need, all under one roof.

Automotive Battery

High-performance batteries for two-wheelers, cars, and heavy-duty vehicles. Our vehicle batteries deliver reliable starting power and longevity under diverse conditions.
